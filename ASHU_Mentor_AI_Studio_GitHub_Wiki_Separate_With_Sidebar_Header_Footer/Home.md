# ASHU Mentor AI Studio Wiki

Welcome to the **ASHU Mentor AI Studio** documentation wiki.

**ASHU Mentor AI Studio** is a local-first AI interview, evaluation, and adaptive training platform that combines resume-aware questioning, JD-aware interview simulation, candidate performance capture, consent-based voice generation, digital-human teaching, lip-sync video rendering, cached lecture prefetching, and downloadable evaluation/report bundles.

## About ASHU Mentor AI Studio

ASHU Mentor AI Studio helps users conduct structured interview practice, generate adaptive training lessons, create digital-human teaching videos, and produce evaluation evidence locally. It is designed for interview coaching, technical training, candidate readiness, and controlled AI presenter demonstrations.

**Tagline:** Local-first digital-human interview and adaptive training studio.
