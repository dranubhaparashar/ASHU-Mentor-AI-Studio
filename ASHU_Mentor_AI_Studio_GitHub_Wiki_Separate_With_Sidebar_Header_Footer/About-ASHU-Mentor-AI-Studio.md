# About ASHU Mentor AI Studio

**ASHU Mentor AI Studio** is a local-first digital-human interview and adaptive training studio.

It combines interview intelligence, candidate evaluation, adaptive learning, consent-based voice generation, and digital presenter videos into a single software platform.

## Project Description

ASHU Mentor AI Studio is an AI-powered interview and training platform designed to simulate structured technical interviews, evaluate candidate responses, generate adaptive learning modules, and deliver personalized video-based teaching through an authorized digital presenter.

The system supports resume and job-description-aware question generation, coding-round evaluation, face-to-face probing, checkpoint-based learning, candidate performance capture, and final report generation.

The platform uses a local LLM for question generation and evaluation, a separate XTTS voice environment for consent-based voice generation, and SadTalker/Wav2Lip-based rendering for AI presenter video generation. It also includes background audio-video rendering, cached lecture prefetching, browser-safe video conversion, YouTube-style playback controls, and downloadable evidence/report bundles.
